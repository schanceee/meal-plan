Vegetarian Starter C — Made on Sunday Sat lunch · Sun lunch

Carrot soup · ginger · coconut milk · coriander

Servings4–6
Active10 min (carrots already roasted)
Storage4 days in the fridge

Ingredients — 4–6 servings

  • 6 roasted carrots from prep
  • 3–4 cm fresh ginger grated
  • 200ml coconut milk
  • 700ml vegetable stock
  • 1 lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Carrots already roasted from Sunday prep. Otherwise, roast at 200°C for 30 min.
  2. Blend carrots + hot stock + grated ginger. Blend at length.
  3. Add coconut milk. Blend until very smooth.
  4. Reheat gently. Add lemon juice gradually. Adjust seasoning.
  5. Serve with fresh coriander and a drizzle of oil. Option: toasted sunflower seeds for crunch.
  6. Storage: 4 days in the fridge. Thin with stock if too thick when reheating.
