Miso soup · leek · wakame · silken tofu

Servings4–6 bowls
Cook10 min
StorageBase 4 days (miso at serving time)

Ingredients — 4–6 bowls

  • 1.5L light vegetable stock or dashi
  • 2 leeks finely sliced
  • 30g dried wakame
  • 300g silken tofu in cubes
  • 4 tbsp white miso
  • A few drops sesame oil optional
  • Spring onion or wild garlic sliced

Absolute rule: Miso must never boil. Heat destroys lactic acid bacteria. Always stir in off the heat, into broth at ~70°C.

Instructions

  1. Rehydrate wakame in cold water for 5 min. Drain.
  2. Bring stock to a gentle simmer. Add leeks. Cook 5 min.
  3. Add wakame and silken tofu. Heat 2 min over very low heat.
  4. Remove from heat. Dissolve white miso in a ladle of hot broth. Pour into the pot. Stir gently. Do not heat again.
  5. Serve immediately with sesame oil and spring onion/wild garlic.
  6. Sunday prep: make the base (stock + leeks + wakame) in large quantity. Add tofu + miso just before serving.
