Braised chicken thighs · white wine · Savoy cabbage · whole-grain mustard

Servings4 (+ lunch leftovers)
Prep10 min
Cook45 min

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 4 chicken thighs ~1.2 kg
  • 1 Savoy cabbage ~800g
  • 200ml dry white wine
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 3 shallots sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves crushed
  • 2 tbsp whole-grain mustard
  • 100ml thick crème fraîche
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 sprigs thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Season thighs skin-side with salt. Heat oil over high heat. Sear thighs 8 min skin-side down without touching, until well golden. Flip, 2 min. Remove.
  2. Same casserole, medium heat: sweat shallots and garlic for 3 min.
  3. Deglaze with white wine, scraping up the fond. Add apple cider vinegar, thyme and bay leaves.
  4. Return thighs. Cover, braise over low heat 25 min.
  5. Add roughly sliced Savoy cabbage. Stir, cover, cook another 12–15 min.
  6. Off the heat: stir in mustard and crème fraîche. Do not boil again. Adjust seasoning.
  7. Serve with sourdough bread for mopping up.
