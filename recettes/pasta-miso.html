Vegetarian White miso Monday · Dinner

Pasta caramelized onions · white miso · Gruyère · crispy sage

Servings4 (+ lunch leftovers)
Prep5 min
Cook50 min (incl. 45 min onions)
Active15 min (if onions from prep)

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 500g pasta (rigatoni or tagliatelle)
  • 8 yellow onions finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp white miso
  • 150g AOP Gruyère grated
  • 20 fresh sage leaves
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g butter
  • Pasta cooking water reserved (200ml)
  • Salt, black pepper

⚑ Miso: always stir in off the heat to preserve probiotics.

Instructions

  1. Melt butter and oil over very low heat. Add onions with a pinch of salt. Caramelize 40–45 min, stirring regularly. (Done during Sunday prep if you followed the plan.)
  2. Cook pasta al dente. Reserve 200 ml of cooking water before draining.
  3. Fry sage leaves in 2 tbsp of hot oil, 30–60 sec until crispy. Place on paper towel.
  4. Off the heat, dissolve white miso in a ladle of hot cooking water, then stir into the onions.
  5. Add drained pasta. Pour cooking water in gradually, stirring to create a silky, glossy sauce.
  6. Stir in grated Gruyère off the heat. Mix until melted. Adjust salt and pepper.
  7. Serve immediately with crispy sage leaves on top.
