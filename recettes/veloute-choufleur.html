Roasted cauliflower velouté · white miso · hazelnuts · goat cheese toast

Servings4
Prep10 min
Cook55 min

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 1 whole cauliflower ~1 kg
  • 2 leeks sliced
  • 2 potatoes diced
  • 2 tbsp white miso
  • 1L vegetable stock
  • 50g hazelnuts
  • 2 tbsp hazelnut oil
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh goat cheese for toasts
  • Sourdough bread

⚑ Miso: stir in off the heat only.

Instructions

  1. Roast the whole cauliflower brushed with oil + salt at 200°C for 40 min. Set aside a few florets for garnish.
  2. Sweat leeks 5 min. Add diced potatoes.
  3. Add stock. Cook 10 min.
  4. Add chopped roasted cauliflower. Blend at length until very smooth.
  5. Off the heat: dissolve white miso in a ladle of soup, stir in. Do not heat again.
  6. Toast hazelnuts dry for 4–5 min. Crush roughly.
  7. Serve with reserved roasted florets, hazelnuts, hazelnut oil. Goat cheese toasts on the side.
