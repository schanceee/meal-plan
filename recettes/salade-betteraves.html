Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 4 roasted beets quartered, from prep
  • 200g cooked green lentils from prep
  • 200g cooked farro from prep
  • 150g fresh goat cheese
  • 100g walnuts
  • 1 orange zest + juice
  • 4 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • Lamb's lettuce or rocket optional

Instructions

  1. Toast walnuts dry for 3–4 min until golden.
  2. Warm beets + lentils + farro together in a pan, 3–4 min.
  3. Dressing: tahini + orange juice + tamari + apple cider vinegar. Thin if too thick.
  4. Assemble in a dish: grains + lentils as base, beets on top, orange zest.
  5. Crumble goat cheese, scatter walnuts.
  6. Drizzle with dressing. Serve warm.
