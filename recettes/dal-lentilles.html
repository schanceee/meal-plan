Lentil Dal · cumin tadka · cold kefir · basmati rice

Servings4 (+ lunch leftovers)
Prep5 min
Cook35 min

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 400g cooked green lentils (from prep)
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes 400g
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 300g basmati rice
  • 500ml kefir or fermented yogurt
  • Tadka
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  • 2 dried chilies

⚑ The tadka is poured at the last moment, over the hot Dal, just before serving. It's irreversible — prepare plates first.

Instructions

  1. Cook the basmati rice. Keep warm.
  2. Sauté the onion in oil for 5 min. Add garlic, 1 min.
  3. Add cumin, coriander, turmeric. Sauté 1 min, stirring.
  4. Add tomatoes. Cook 10 min over medium heat.
  5. Stir in lentils and tamari. Heat 5 min. Keep warm over very low heat.
  6. Tadka — do just before serving: heat butter over high heat. As soon as it foams, add cumin seeds + chilies. Wait 30–45 sec until seeds pop. Pour immediately over the Dal — it will sizzle.
  7. Serve over rice with cold kefir on the side. Do not mix kefir into the hot Dal.
