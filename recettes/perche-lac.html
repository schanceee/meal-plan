Lake Geneva perch · wild garlic butter · spinach · crushed potatoes

Servings4 (+ lunch leftovers)
Prep5 min
Cook30 min

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 4 fillets Lake Geneva perch with skin, or trout
  • 800g potatoes
  • 300g fresh spinach
  • 50g butter
  • 3 tbsp wild garlic oil (from prep)
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 lemon
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Sea salt, black pepper

⚑ The pan must be very hot before adding the fish. Skin-side only for 4 min — do not touch.

Instructions

  1. Cook potatoes in their skins for 25 min. Peel, crush with olive oil and salt.
  2. Pat fillets dry with paper towel. Season skin-side with salt.
  3. Very hot pan + oil. Place fillets skin-side down. Press for 10 seconds. Cook 4 min without touching.
  4. Flip, cook 45 sec to 1 min. Remove to a plate.
  5. In the same pan off the heat: butter until golden brown. Add wild garlic oil + apple cider vinegar. Pour over fillets.
  6. Sauté spinach 2 min over high heat + lemon + salt.
  7. Serve immediately.
