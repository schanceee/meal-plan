Thin leek tart · AOP Gruyère · crème fraîche

Servings4
Prep10 min
Cook45 min

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 1 roll puff pastry
  • 4 leeks ~600g, sliced
  • 100g AOP Gruyère grated
  • 100ml thick crème fraîche
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, nutmeg
  • Green salad to accompany
  • Whole-grain mustard for the dressing

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. Melt leeks in oil over low heat, 15 min. Deglaze with apple cider vinegar. Let evaporate 1 min.
  3. Mix crème fraîche + eggs + nutmeg + salt + pepper.
  4. Roll out pastry, prick with a fork. Leave a 1.5 cm border.
  5. Mix leeks + filling, pour over pastry. Sprinkle with Gruyère.
  6. Bake 25–30 min until golden and crispy.
  7. Leave to cool 5 min. Serve with green salad and mustard-cider dressing.
