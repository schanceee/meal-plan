Vegetarian Tamari Assembly from prep Tuesday · Dinner

Grain bowl · roasted vegetables · lentils · tahini-tamari dressing

Servings4 (+ lunch leftovers)
Active10 min (all from prep)

Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 300g cooked farro or spelt (from prep)
  • 200g cooked green lentils (from prep)
  • 4 roasted beets quartered (from prep)
  • 4 roasted carrots (from prep)
  • 200g roasted celeriac (from prep)
  • 200g fresh spinach
  • 4 tbsp tahini
  • 2 lemons juice
  • 2 tbsp tamari
  • 1 garlic clove grated
  • 4 tbsp sunflower seeds toasted
  • Wild garlic oil (from prep)
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Warm farro and lentils in a pan with a splash of water or stock, 3–4 min over medium heat.
  2. Wilt the spinach in the same pan with a drop of oil, 1–2 min.
  3. Make the dressing: tahini + lemon juice + tamari + grated garlic. Thin with hot water spoonful by spoonful until creamy.
  4. Warm the roasted vegetables if needed.
  5. Assemble in bowls: farro + lentils as base, roasted vegetables on top, spinach on the side.
  6. Drizzle with dressing, sunflower seeds, drizzle of wild garlic oil to finish.
