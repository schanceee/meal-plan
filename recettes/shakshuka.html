Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 2 cans crushed tomatoes 800g
  • 2 red peppers roasted and peeled
  • 6 eggs
  • 150g feta
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp harissa
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 3 tbsp wild garlic oil
  • Sourdough bread to serve

Instructions

  1. Roast peppers under the grill or over a flame until charred. Peel under water or after 10 min in a closed bag.
  2. Sauté onion 5 min in oil. Add garlic, 1 min.
  3. Add smoked paprika + cumin. Sauté 1 min.
  4. Add tomatoes + sliced roasted peppers + harissa. Simmer covered for 15 min.
  5. Make 6 wells. Crack one egg into each.
  6. Cover and cook: 8 min for runny yolks, 10 min for medium.
  7. Crumble feta. Drizzle with wild garlic oil. Serve in the pan with sourdough bread.
