Ingredients — 4 servings

  • 200g fresh lamb's lettuce
  • 1 bunch pink radishes finely sliced
  • 4 tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar unfiltered
  • 1 tbsp whole-grain mustard
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 shallot finely diced
  • Salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast sunflower seeds dry for 3 min. Set aside.
  2. Dressing: apple cider vinegar + mustard + shallot + salt. Emulsify with oil.
  3. Wash and dry lamb's lettuce gently.
  4. Dress at the last moment. Add radishes and seeds.
  5. Serve immediately.
