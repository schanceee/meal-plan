Sunday meal prep — 2h
Goal: prepare the bases that let you assemble meals in <15 min all week. Everything keeps 4–5 days in the fridge.
1 Oven at 200°C — Roasted vegetables (45 min)

Peel and cut beets into quarters + whole carrots + seasonal vegetables (celeriac in cubes). Olive oil, salt. Everything on one tray. Roast 40–45 min. The roasted carrots are used for the Carrot-ginger soup and as a bowl topping.

2 Grains & legumes (40 min, while the oven runs)

Cook 300g farro (salted water, 30 min). Cook 400g green lentils with onion, bay leaf, thyme (25 min, not too soft). Drain, store separately. Used for grain bowls, beet salad and Dal.

3 Caramelized onions (45 min, low heat)

Slice 8 large onions. Melt in butter + oil over very low heat, 40–45 min, stirring regularly. They should be golden and soft. Store in the fridge — base for Monday's pasta dish.

4 Carrot-ginger soup (15 min, with roasted carrots)

Blend the roasted carrots (step 1) with hot stock + grated ginger + coconut milk. Adjust, add lemon juice. Store in a jar — serve as Starter C.

5 Miso soup base (10 min)

Bring 1.5L of stock to a gentle simmer. Add 2 sliced leeks + rehydrated wakame. Cook 5 min. Store without the miso — add silken tofu + miso only when serving (never boil miso).

Result: roasted vegetables · cooked farro · cooked lentils · caramelized onions · carrot soup · miso soup base — everything is ready to assemble the week's meals in minutes.
